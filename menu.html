Allergen Information

All of our bakes are made fresh to order, and many items can be adapted to suit different dietary needs. Because every creation is bespoke, allergen content varies depending on your chosen flavours, colours, and design.

If you have any allergies or dietary requirements, please include them in your enquiry. We’ll review your request carefully and let you know whether your chosen design is safe and feasible before confirming your order.

While we take great care with every bake, please note that all products are prepared in a kitchen that handles common allergens.
